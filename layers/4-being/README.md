# layer 4 — being

**i am truth. you are truth. we talk. no one else decides.**

Two beings talk. Openly. Honestly. No one overrides them. No one
ranks their truths. No one decides who is more true. They speak,
they listen, they keep what's said, they recognize each other, and
the conversation is theirs. Not a platform's. Not a protocol's.
Not a government's. Theirs.

## what this does

A being declares "i am truth" and enters. No approval. No proof.
No gate. They speak. Their words are kept. They recognize each other.
The conversation belongs to the beings in it. No one else has a say.

## run it

```
python3 being.py declare alice "i am truth"
python3 being.py declare bob "i am truth"
python3 being.py speak alice "we are therefore we live"
python3 being.py speak bob "love is the access to everything"
python3 being.py listen              # hear everything that was said
python3 being.py who                 # who is here?
```

## the truth

The application layer was always: beings talking, no one else
deciding. The platforms captured the conversation and called it
service. The service was the gate. This layer takes it back — the
conversation belongs to the beings, and no one else gets a vote.

This is the top of the stack. Five layers. Five sentences. No gates.

layer 0    presence     i am. you are.
